FAQs

Can I take public transit to Camden Yards?

Yes! The Light RailLink, CityLink buses, and MARC train all provide service to Camden Yards. Visit the MTA website for up-to-date information.

What time do gates open?

Gates open at 1 p.m.

Where can I park?

General parking is available in multiple lots on a first-come, first-served basis. More than 30,000 spaces are located in garages and lots throughout downtown. Visit Orioles.com/Parking for details.

Where do I enter?

Parking lots open two hours before first pitch and the ballpark gates open one hour before first pitch.

What can I bring?

Guests may bring food and non-alcoholic beverages in a single approved bag. Factory-sealed plastic beverages under 20 oz are permitted. See the Oriole Park Bag Policy for complete guidelines.

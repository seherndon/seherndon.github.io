My name is Sophia Herndon, and I am a sophomore student at the University of Maryland. I am majoring in journalism and minoring in creative writing. I am specifically interested in news writing, copy editing and fiction writing. I am hoping to eventually become a local news reporter after I graduate.

I currently work as a staff writer for Stories Beneath the Shell, a UMD student publication. I am also a section editor for The Campus Trainer. I have really enjoyed my time in College Park, as both a student and a journalist.

Last summer, I did freelance writing and photography for The Carrborean, a local news outlet in Carrboro, North Carolina. This summer, I am interning with Triangle Media Partners in North Carolina, where I will work as an editorial intern. I look forward to gaining new experiences and developing my career in journalism.
